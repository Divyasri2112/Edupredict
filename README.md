# Edupredict
 EduPredict is your one-step platform for discovering the best academic and career opportunities after every stage of education — from 10th to post-graduation.  
      We aim to simplify your journey by providing verified exam information, smart guidance, and helpful resources — all in one place.
Mission
    Empower students with clear, reliable, and updated educational pathways for a brighter future.
    We bring all exams, courses, and career options together, making it easier for students to choose confidently.
